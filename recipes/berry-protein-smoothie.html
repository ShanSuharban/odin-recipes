Berry Protein Smoothie

Description

A nutrient-dense , high-protein smoothie designed for entrepreneurs who need sustained energy, mental clarity, and peak performance throughout the day. Packed with antioxidants, protein, fiber, and healthy fats , this smoothie fuels your body and brain without the sugar crash.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries ( blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, or blackberries )
  • 1 scoop protein powder ( whey, plant-based, or collagen )
  • ½ banana
  • 1 tbsp chia or flax seeds
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt (or almond milk for dairy-free option)
  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ½ cup water or ice cubes

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Wash the berries and measure all ingredients.
  2. Blend: Add all ingredients into a blender.
  3. Mix Until Smooth: Blend for 30-45 seconds until creamy and well-mixed.
  4. Adjust Consistency: Add more water or ice if needed and blend again
  5. Serve & Enjoy: Pour into a glass and fuel your productivity!
