Honey Glazed Chicken Wings

Description

These baked honey glazed chicken wings are the perfect blend of sweet and savory. Packed full of vibrant Asian flavors, these chicken wings are finger licking good! Coated in a honey and soy glaze, you’re going to love these tangy wings!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
    • 2 pounds skin-on chicken wings bone-in
    • 3 egg whites
    • ½ tsp paprika
    • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Glaze
    • ½ cup honey
    • ¼ soy sauce
    • 1 inch knob fresh ginger grated
    • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • Juice of 1 lime
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil
    • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 400F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or baking paper. Place an oven safe cooling rack on top and spray it with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Wash chicken wings, then pat them dry with kitchen towel paper. Place chicken wings on the cooling rack (2 pounds skin-on chicken wings bone-in ).
  3. In a bowl, whisk the egg whites with the salt, black pepper and paprika until frothy (3 egg whites, ¼ tsp salt, ½ tsp paprika, ½ tsp black pepper ).
  4. Brush egg white mixture on chicken wings on both sides.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes. Remember to flip them half-way through so they bake evenly.
  6. Brush egg white mixture on chicken wings on both sides
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes. Remember to flip them half-way through so they bake evenly
  8. While they’re baking, prepare the glossy glaze.
  9. In a saucepan add the honey, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, ginger, juice of lime, ground coriander, red pepper flakes and sesame oil. Mix well.
  10. Bring to boil, then simmer until sauce is reduced and becomes sticky (½ cup honey, ¼ soy sauce, 1 inch knob fresh ginger grated, 1 tsp red pepper flakes, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce, Juice of 1 lime, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1 tbsp sesame oil ).
  11. Once chicken wings are cooked let them cool for 5 minutes. Add them in a bowl, then coat with the glaze
  12. Sprinkle some sesame seeds, fresh cilantro and enjoy! (1 tbsp sesame seeds, 1 tbsp fresh cilantro chopped )
